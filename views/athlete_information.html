Fun Fact!!

Dipa Karmakar, born in 1993, represented India at the 2016 Summer Olympics. She attained 4th position in the Women's Vault Gymnastics, and was the first in India to do so. She started practicing at the age of 6 in an under-resourced environment, and uses her resources to promote gymnastics for girls in India.
